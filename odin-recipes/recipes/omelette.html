Egg White omelette

Description

This egg white omelette is a light, fluffy, and healthy meal, featuring minimal vegetables and fresh herbs for a subtle flavor. Perfectly cooked with a clean, white appearance, it offers a protein-packed, low-calorie option for a nutritious breakfast or snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 egg whites
  • A handful of finely chopped spinach or bell peppers (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or chives for garnish
  • Olive oil or cooking spray for the pan

Steps

  1. Lightly grease a non-stick pan with olive oil or cooking spray and heat over medium-low heat.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the egg whites with a pinch of salt and pepper until slightly frothy.
  3. Pour the egg whites into the heated pan, tilting the pan to spread the mixture evenly.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is set and the edges start to lift.
  5. Add any finely chopped vegetables (like spinach or bell peppers) on one half of the omelette.
  6. Gently fold the other half of the omelette over the filling using a spatula.
  7. Cook for another minute until fully cooked through, then slide onto a plate.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley or chives and serve warm.
